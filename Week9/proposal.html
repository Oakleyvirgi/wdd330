Young Women Attendance List

Purpose

To create a website where the Young Women's Attendance will be taken. This will be useful for reporting to the ward/s clerk the girls attendance and for ensuring attendance is taken whether or not the Young Women Secretary is present. Young Women will check their name off the list each Sunday. The site will keep record of the girls attendance and generate trimester report on the attendance. The site should also allow new girls or visitors to enter their names. Attending girls camp will also be included in the site as it is also needed to be reported to the ward clerk. When the Young women enter or check off their names either a song or message will display to make them feel special about attending

Audience

The audience for this website is going to be the Young Women, ages 11-17, who attend church or camp meeting within the Burlington 1st Ward, the Young Women Presidency (President, First Counselor, Second Counselor, and Secretary) will have access to the site to view the attendance and report it to the bishop

Data sources

LocalStorage will definitely need to be implemented, for it will be needing to keep record of the attendance to generate the reports. Either using a JSON file to display the scripture or church music to be displayed after the girls have signed up their attendance.

Initial Module list

A module to collect and keep record of the girls attendance. Another module to do the calculation and report of the girls attendance individually and as a organization. And I think another module to play a song or show the scriptural message.
